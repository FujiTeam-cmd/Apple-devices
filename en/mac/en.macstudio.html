Announcements and Release Dates

  • First Mac Studio: March 8, 2022
  • Mac Studio with M1 Ultra: March 8, 2022

Key Features

  • Apple M1 and M2 Ultra chips
  • Support for up to 128GB of unified memory
  • 4K, 5K, and 6K display support
  • Compact and fanless design
  • Multiple Thunderbolt 4 and USB-C ports
  • Up to 8TB of storage

Performance and Use Cases

Mac Studio is built for professionals in creative fields such as video editing, 3D rendering, music production, and software development. It delivers incredible performance in a compact form factor, providing a workstation-grade experience for demanding tasks.

Reviews and Popularity

The Mac Studio is praised for its exceptional power and performance, especially for its size. It is considered a top choice for creators, designers, and professionals who need serious computing power without the bulk of traditional desktops.
