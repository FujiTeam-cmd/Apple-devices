iMac Series

History

iMac — All-in-One Powerhouse

Announcements and Release Dates

  • First iMac: August 15, 1998
  • iMac (Intel): January 7, 2009
  • iMac (Retina 4K & 5K): October 13, 2015
  • iMac (M1): April 20, 2021

Key Features

  • Apple M1 chip (for 2021 model)
  • 24-inch and 27-inch Retina 4K/5K displays
  • Up to 16GB RAM, 1TB SSD storage
  • Thin and elegant design with a variety of color options
  • Up to 10-core CPU and 8-core GPU

Performance and Use Cases

The iMac is perfect for creatives, professionals, and home users who need powerful computing in an elegant, space-saving all-in-one form factor.

Reviews and Popularity

Praised for its stunning display, sleek design, and powerful performance. The iMac remains a top choice for users who want a reliable, stylish desktop computer.
