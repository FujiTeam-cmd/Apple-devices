MacBook Pro Series

History

MacBook Pro — Power for Professionals

Announcements and Release Dates

  • First MacBook Pro: January 10, 2006
  • MacBook Pro (M1): November 10, 2020
  • MacBook Pro (14/16-inch, M1 Pro/Max): October 18, 2021
  • MacBook Pro (M3 Series): October 30, 2023

Key Features

  • Apple M1, M2, and M3 chip options
  • 14-inch and 16-inch Liquid Retina XDR displays
  • ProMotion (120Hz refresh rate)
  • Multiple Thunderbolt 4 ports, HDMI, SD card slot
  • Up to 22 hours battery life

Performance and Use Cases

Built for creative professionals, developers, and power users, the MacBook Pro offers desktop-class performance in a portable form factor.

Reviews and Popularity

Highly rated for its incredible performance, display quality, and port variety. A favorite among video editors, coders, and music producers.
