Release Dates and Announcements

  • MacBook (1st Generation): 16 May 2006
  • MacBook (12-inch, 1st Generation): 9 March 2015
  • MacBook (M1, 2020): 10 November 2020

Main Features

  • Apple M1 or Intel Core processors (depending on the model)
  • Retina Display (12-inch option)
  • Magic Keyboard
  • USB-C/Thunderbolt ports
  • Long battery life

Performance and Versatility

The MacBook offers a slim design and portability while providing excellent performance with Apple's M1 chip. It is ideal for users who need a lightweight laptop for everyday tasks, light professional work, and entertainment.

Reviews and Popularity

MacBook is praised for its elegant design, fast performance, and efficient battery life. It’s a preferred option for students, professionals, and creatives who appreciate a minimalist, high-performance laptop.
