MacBook Air Series

History

MacBook Air — Lightweight Performance

Announcements and Release Dates

  • First MacBook Air: January 15, 2008
  • MacBook Air (M1): November 10, 2020
  • MacBook Air (M2): June 6, 2022
  • MacBook Air (15-inch): June 5, 2023

Key Features

  • Apple M1 and M2 chips
  • 13-inch and 15-inch Liquid Retina displays
  • MagSafe charging, Thunderbolt / USB 4
  • Thin and lightweight design (up to 1.2 kg)
  • Up to 18 hours of battery life

Ease of Use

The MacBook Air is ideal for students, travelers, and everyday users. Its light design and powerful chip make it a top choice without compromising performance.

Reviews and Popularity

Praised for its silent performance, long battery life, and high-quality display, the MacBook Air is considered one of the most balanced Apple laptops by users and reviewers.
