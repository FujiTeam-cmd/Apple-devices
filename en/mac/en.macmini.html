Mac Mini Series

History

Mac Mini — Compact Power

Announcements and Release Dates

  • First Mac Mini: January 11, 2005
  • Mac Mini (Intel): March 1, 2006
  • Mac Mini (M1): November 10, 2020
  • Mac Mini (M2): January 17, 2023

Key Features

  • Apple M1 and M2 chips (for 2020 and 2023 models)
  • Compact design (7.7 inches square and 1.4 inches tall)
  • Up to 64GB RAM and 2TB SSD storage
  • Thunderbolt / USB 4, HDMI 2.0, Gigabit Ethernet
  • Supports up to three displays (for M1 version)

Performance and Use Cases

The Mac Mini is a small but powerful machine, suitable for home users, professionals, and developers who need strong performance in a compact form factor.

Reviews and Popularity

Known for its impressive performance in such a small package, the Mac Mini is a favorite among users who want a powerful desktop without taking up too much space.
