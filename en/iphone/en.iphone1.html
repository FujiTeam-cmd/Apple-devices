History

iPhone 1 — Creation History

Announcement and Release Date

  • Announcement: January 9, 2007, Macworld Conference & Expo, San Francisco
  • Release Date: June 29, 2007
  • Key Speakers: Steve Jobs

Model Overview

  • 3.5" display with 320x480 resolution
  • Samsung ARM 1176 chip
  • 2MP camera
  • GSM and EDGE network support
  • Touchscreen and touch-based interface

Key Features

  • First model with a touchscreen and no physical buttons
  • Safari web browser integration
  • Intuitive iOS operating system (then iPhone OS)

Reviews and Reception

iPhone 1 was a revolutionary device that changed the mobile phone industry. The touchscreen, lack of physical buttons, and internet access were met with enthusiasm. However, the limited camera features and absence of 3G received some criticism.
