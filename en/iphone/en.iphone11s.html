Announcement and Release Date

  • Announcement: September 10, 2019, Apple Park

Lineup Models

  • iPhone 11 — 6.1", base model
  • iPhone 11 Pro — 5.8", premium
  • iPhone 11 Pro Max — 6.5", flagship

Key Innovations

  • A13 Bionic chip
  • Dual camera system (iPhone 11)
  • Triple camera with Night Mode (Pro models)
  • Improved battery life
  • New color options (including green and purple)

Reviews and Reception

The iPhone 11 was praised for value and performance. The Pro models were celebrated for their cameras and the Super Retina XDR display.
