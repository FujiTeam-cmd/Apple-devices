iPhone 15 series

History

iPhone 15 Series — Creation History

Announcement and Release Date

  • Announcement: September 12, 2023, Apple Park, Cupertino
  • Release Date: September 22, 2023
  • Key Speakers: Tim Cook, Johny Srouji, Craig Federighi

Lineup Models

  • iPhone 15 — 6.1", base model
  • iPhone 15 Plus — 6.7", larger version
  • iPhone 15 Pro — 6.1", premium model
  • iPhone 15 Pro Max — 6.7", top-tier model

Key Innovations

  • A16 (15/15 Plus) and A17 Pro (Pro models) chips
  • USB-C port replaces Lightning
  • Titanium frame on Pro models
  • Dynamic Island on all models
  • New 5x periscope zoom on Pro Max

Apple Intelligence

The iPhone 15 series introduced early AI integration, mostly in the form of camera processing and on-device machine learning. Full AI suite came later with iPhone 16.

Source: MacRumors

Cameras

  • 48MP main sensor (all models)
  • 3x telephoto on Pro, 5x periscope on Pro Max
  • Photonic Engine improvements

Source: The Verge

Performance

  • A16 chip in base models
  • A17 Pro chip in Pro models
  • Support for hardware-accelerated ray tracing (Pro only)

Source: Wikipedia

Reviews and Reception

Praised for camera improvements, USB-C adoption, and lighter Pro models. Some critiques on pricing and limited changes for non-Pro models.

Box Contents and Accessories
