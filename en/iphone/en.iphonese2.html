Model Overview

  • 4.7" Retina HD display
  • A13 Bionic chip
  • 12MP camera with 4K video support
  • Touch ID for unlocking
  • Thin aluminum and glass body

Key Features

  • Performance similar to iPhone 11
  • Compact and affordable
  • 4.7" display and improved camera

Reviews and Reception

The iPhone SE (2nd Gen) was seen as an excellent choice for those seeking a powerful smartphone in a compact body at a more affordable price. The camera, performance, and battery life were highly praised.
