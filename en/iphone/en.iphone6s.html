iPhone 6 — Creation History

Announcement and Release Date

  • Announcement: September 9, 2014, Flint Center, Cupertino
  • Release Date: September 19, 2014
  • Key Speakers: Tim Cook, Phil Schiller

Model Overview

  • 4.7" Retina HD display
  • A8 chip
  • 8MP camera with enhanced optical image stabilization
  • Supports 4G LTE
  • Thin design with aluminum body

Key Features

  • Larger screen size (4.7" and 5.5" for iPhone 6 Plus)
  • New A8 chip for better performance
  • Available in two sizes: iPhone 6 and iPhone 6 Plus

Reviews and Reception

The iPhone 6 received positive reviews for its improved performance and larger screen but also faced criticism for the "Bendgate" issue where the phone was prone to bending under pressure.
