Announcement and Release Date

  • Announcement: September 9, 2024, Apple Park, Cupertino
  • Release Date: September 20, 2024
  • Key Speakers: Tim Cook, Johny Srouji, Craig Federighi

Lineup Models

  • iPhone 16 — 6.1", base model
  • iPhone 16 Plus — 6.7", larger version
  • iPhone 16 Pro — 6.3", premium features
  • iPhone 16 Pro Max — 6.9", flagship with top specs

Key Innovations

  • New A18 and A18 Pro chips
  • Capture Button for instant photo/video
  • Support for Spatial Photos and Videos
  • Universal USB-C port
  • Wi-Fi 7 and new colors

Apple Intelligence

The iPhone 16 series features advanced AI capabilities: improved Siri, Genmoji, Image Playground, and more. Some features are rolling out gradually until March 2025.

Cameras

  • 48MP main sensor
  • 5x telephoto lens (Pro Max only)
  • Physical Camera Control button

Performance

  • A18 chip with 6-core CPU

  • Ray tracing support in Pro models

Critics and users praised the iPhone 16 lineup, especially the new camera features and performance. Some AI-powered features are still being gradually released.
