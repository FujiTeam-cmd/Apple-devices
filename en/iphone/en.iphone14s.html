Lineup Models

  • iPhone 14 — 6.1", base model
  • iPhone 14 Plus — 6.7", larger version
  • iPhone 14 Pro — 6.1", premium
  • iPhone 14 Pro Max — 6.7", flagship

Key Innovations

  • Dynamic Island (Pro models)
  • New 48MP camera (Pro models)
  • Crash Detection feature
  • Satellite SOS for emergency communication
  • A15 Bionic (14/14 Plus), A16 Bionic (Pro)

Reviews and Reception

The iPhone 14 was praised for its improved cameras and safety features, but base models were criticized for being too similar to iPhone 13.
