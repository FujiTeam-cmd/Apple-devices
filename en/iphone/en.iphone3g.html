iPhone 3G — Creation History

Announcement and Release Date

  • Announcement: June 9, 2008, WWDC 2008, San Francisco
  • Release Date: July 11, 2008
  • Key Speakers: Steve Jobs

Model Overview

  • 3.5" display with 320x480 resolution
  • ARM 11 chip
  • 2MP camera
  • 3G network support
  • Wi-Fi and Bluetooth

Key Features

  • 3G and GPS support
  • Improved performance compared to the original iPhone
  • Available in 8GB and 16GB versions

Reviews and Reception

The iPhone 3G was hailed as a revolutionary device due to its 3G network support and improved features compared to the first iPhone. However, some users were disappointed by the lack of autofocus camera and the pricing of the model in the US.
