Model Overview

  • 3.5" display with 320x480 resolution
  • ARM Cortex-A8 chip
  • 3MP camera with autofocus
  • Video recording support
  • Improved performance over iPhone 3G

Key Features

  • Improved camera with autofocus and video recording
  • Faster performance thanks to the ARM Cortex-A8 chip
  • Support for Voice Control

Reviews and Reception

The iPhone 3GS received positive reviews for its improved performance and camera capabilities. The autofocus camera and video recording feature were highly praised, along with the enhanced speed of the device.
