iPad mini Series

History

iPad mini — Compact and Powerful

Announcements and Release Dates

  • iPad mini (1st generation): October 23, 2012
  • iPad mini 2: October 22, 2013
  • iPad mini 3: October 16, 2014
  • iPad mini 4: September 9, 2015
  • iPad mini (5th generation): March 18, 2019
  • iPad mini (6th generation): September 14, 2021

Key Features

  • A15 Bionic chip (6th generation)
  • 8.3-inch Liquid Retina display
  • Touch ID in the power button
  • Support for Apple Pencil (2nd generation)
  • USB-C port

Form Factor and Usability

The iPad mini is valued for its portability, lightweight design, and powerful performance in a compact body. It's ideal for reading, taking notes, and travel.

Reviews and Usage

Users and critics note that the iPad mini combines the performance of larger models with the convenience of a smartphone. It’s a versatile tool for students, doctors, and travelers alike.

Package Contents and Accessories

  • Included: iPad mini, USB-C cable, power adapter
  • Optional: Apple Pencil (2nd generation), cases
