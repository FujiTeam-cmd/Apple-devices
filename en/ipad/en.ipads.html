Announcements and Release Dates

  • iPad (1st generation): January 27, 2010
  • iPad (5th generation): March 21, 2017
  • iPad (6th generation): March 27, 2018
  • iPad (7th generation): September 10, 2019
  • iPad (8th generation): September 15, 2020
  • iPad (9th generation): September 14, 2021
  • iPad (10th generation): October 18, 2022

Key Features

  • A13 or A14 Bionic chip (depending on generation)
  • 10.2 or 10.9-inch Retina display
  • Touch ID
  • Support for Apple Pencil (1st generation)
  • USB-C (from 10th generation)

Affordability and Versatility

The iPad is Apple’s most affordable tablet, perfect for education, everyday use, work, and entertainment. Its simplicity makes it ideal for beginners.

Reviews and Popularity

The classic iPad remains the most popular choice due to its price, reliability, and familiar interface. It is recommended by schools, families, and individual users alike.
